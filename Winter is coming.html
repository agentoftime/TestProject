Insomnia

The capital city of the Lucis dynasty. The city is protected by a barrier, provided by the crystal. The crystal who has chosen the heir, Noctis, marking him a target by the Imperials.

The city now lays in ruin, thanks to the Empire's deceit.

Hammerhead

An outpost, a short distance outside of the city of Insomnia. This is where Cid's garage is located in order to upgrade your vehicle, The Regalia, and some weapons.

There is a diner there so food can be eaten, giving the party stat increases for possible ensuing battles. There is also a camper so the party can sleep in, gaining some extra EXP for spending the night there.

Qaldin Quay

A port village. A great spot for Noctis to partake in his favorite past-time (but certainly not yours in the game), fishing. There is a camper outside of port but if you stay at the (very) expensive hotel, you get triple the EXP.

Dino can be found here. Doing his quests amounts in quality gems and EXP, as well as gaining an ally.
